Abacus

Abacus

Abacus is a simple learning instrument with rows of beads in frame used to count and calculate, first originated from China and later Japanese made improvements on the instrument, modified and modernized which is prevalent now in all over the world.
Soraban is the name given to the traditional Japanese Abacus or Calculation form. The traditional Japanese “natural calculation device” has unique advantage in the digital age.
